xose segovia martin

I am interested in the relationship between cognition and society. In particular, I am currently investigating the effects of cognitive biases and network topologies on the evolution of the convergence of cultural conventions. My research uses both computer modelling and experiments in the lab to understand how the interaction between individual biases and social structures, rather than just the aggregate preferences of individual actors, leads to a concrete distribution of languages. I've been involved in a postgraduate research program at the Centre for Language Evolution (University of Edinburgh) and now I spend most of my time working as a PhD student in an interantional research program organised by the Unversitat Autònoma de Barcelona.
