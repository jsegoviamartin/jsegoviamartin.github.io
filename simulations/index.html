These visualizations illustrate the emergence of shared cultrual conventions in 16-agent micro-societies using an agent-based model as described in Segovia Martín, J., Walker, B., Fay, N. & Tamariz, M. (2019). "Network connectivity dynamics affect the evolution of culturally transmitted variants"

Rows represent rounds, columns represent agents and colors represent varaint choice. We show results for three levels of content bias and three levels of population connectivity dynamics.
